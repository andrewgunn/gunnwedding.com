Resort

Kamari, Santorini

We'll be staying in Kamari. We'd love for you to stay nearby but feel free to choose another resort if you'd prefer.

Make sure your accommodation is actually in Kamari if you want to be near the beach, restaurants, and bars. Some hotels claim to be in Kamari but could be a 45 minute walk away (closer to the airport).

There are buses on the island but taxis are sparse so it's better to stay somewhere that has attractions within walking distance depending on the type of holiday you want.

Reservations

Please feel free to book flights and accommodation of your choice. We won't be making a group booking on behalf of everybody.

The majority of accommodation haven't released their 2023 prices so don't worry if you can't find anything. Booking.com and airbnb have a big selection but you'll have to book your flights separately via Jet2, TUI, or easyJet.
