Resort

Kamari, Santorini

We'll be staying in Kamari. We'd love for you to stay nearby but feel free to choose another resort if you'd prefer.

There are buses on the island but taxis are sparse so it's better to stay somewhere that has attractions within walking distance depending on the type of holiday you want.

FAQs

I'm not sure if I can make it yet. What should I do?

That's ok, please RVSP with your best guess for now. We'll ask for final RVSPs 6 months before the wedding, by 30th November 2022.

What will the weather be like?

Warm and dry with temperatures between 21-27°C degrees (70-81°F).

What should I wear?

Smart casual. Ladies, no white please. Men, feel free to wear smart shorts and suit jackets aren't necessary.

Can I bring my kids?

Yes, absolutely! Just let us know on the RVSP.

Can I bring a plus one?

Of course. Just let us know on the RSVP.
